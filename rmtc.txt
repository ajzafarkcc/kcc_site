The Regional Manufacturing Technology Center (RMTC) is an innovative, community-driven training facility in the Fort Custer Industrial Park in Battle Creek, Michigan. The facility is home to KCC's Industrial Trades and Workforce Solutions. Together, these departments design training programs using blended learning techniques.

Training programs are designed to meet the employee training needs of area business and industry. The innovative approach to training enables the RMTC to respond to training needs quickly and efficiently. Training is available to individuals on a walk-in basis or can be scheduled to meet production and service schedules and may be provided either at the worksite, at the RMTC or at any location in the community.

To find out more about training available through the RMTC, please spend a few minutes reviewing this website or contact the RMTC directly at 269-965-4137.
